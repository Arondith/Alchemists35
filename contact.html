Questions, answers

What services does Alchemists&Architects offer?

At Alchemists&Architects, we offer a broad spectrum of services, including precision haircuts, creative color techniques, balayage, highlights, keratin treatments, styling, facials, and makeup applications. We also specialize in event styling for weddings, photo shoots, and other special occasions, ensuring you look and feel your best for any event.

Do you offer discounts for first-time clients?

Yes! We believe in giving new clients a warm welcome. Alchemists&Architects offers a special discount on your first visit, allowing you to experience our high-end services at an introductory price. Contact us for more details on how to redeem this offer!

Can I book an appointment online at Alchemists&Architects?

Absolutely! Booking an appointment at Alchemists&Architects is easy. Simply visit our website, select the service you want, choose your preferred stylist, and pick a time that works best for you. We make it convenient for you to schedule and manage your appointments online.

What sets Alchemists&Architects apart from other salons in Seattle?

Alchemists&Architects blends the art of transformation with architectural precision. Our talented team is dedicated to creating bespoke styles that are a perfect reflection of your personality and preferences. We offer personalized services in a sophisticated, serene setting, using only the finest products and techniques to achieve the look you desire.

Do you offer bridal or event styling services?

Yes, we specialize in luxury bridal and event styling. Whether it’s a wedding, gala, or special occasion, our stylists will work with you to create a flawless look. From elegant updos to stunning makeup, we’ll ensure you feel like your best self for any celebration.

What should I expect during my first visit to Alchemists&Architects?

During your first visit, you’ll enjoy a personalized consultation with one of our expert stylists. They will listen to your ideas and offer recommendations based on your hair and beauty goals. From there, you’ll be treated to a luxurious service designed to give you the exact look you’re envisioning.
